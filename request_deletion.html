Account Deletion Request

If you would like to request the deletion of your account and all associated personal data, please send an email to matthewswinnockdev@gmail.com with the subject line "Request for Account and Data Deletion".

In your email, please provide the following information:

  • Your full name.
  • Your email address used in the app.
  • Confirmation that you wish to delete your account and all associated data.

What Data Will Be Deleted?

Upon account and data deletion, the following will be permanently erased from our systems:

  • Your Google account data used for sign-in (including your email and name).
  • Any data associated with your Google account, such as app usage information collected through Google Sign-In.
  • Any records related to ad interactions through AdMob. We do not store detailed ad data, but any related identifiers or analytics data will be removed.

Once your request is processed, all traces of your account and associated data will be deleted, and we will notify you once this process is complete.
