3. Legal Bases for Processing (GDPR Compliance)

Under GDPR, we rely on the following legal bases for processing your information:

  • Performance of a Contract: To provide the App and its features.
  • Consent: For showing targeted ads, analytics, or sending promotional communications (where applicable).
  • Legitimate Interests: For improving the App and its features.
  • Legal Obligations: For complying with applicable laws.

4. Your GDPR Rights

As a user in the UK or EU, you have the following rights under GDPR:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: Request corrections to any inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure: Request the deletion of your personal data ("Right to be Forgotten"), subject to legal or operational requirements.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request the limitation of how we process your data.
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your personal data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object: Object to the processing of your personal data for specific purposes, such as marketing or analytics.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: If you have given consent for processing, you can withdraw it at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at matthewswinnockdev@gmail.com.

We do not sell your personal information. However, we may share your information with:

  • Legal authorities: If required by law or to protect our rights.

6. Data Storage and Security

We store your data securely and implement measures to protect it from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of data transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6.1 Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to provide our services or comply with legal obligations. Data collected for analytics or advertising purposes is retained as per the policies of third-party services such as Firebase and Google AdMob.

7. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred outside the UK or EU. We ensure such transfers are protected by safeguards, such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs).
